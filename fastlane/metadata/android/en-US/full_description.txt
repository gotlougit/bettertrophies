BetterTrophies is an unofficial, open source Android client for checking
PlayStation profile data, trophy progress, and cloud captures from one place.

Features:
- View your profile, trophy level, points, and overall completion progress.
- Browse the full trophy library and drill into a game's earned and missing
  trophies.
- Browse PlayStation cloud captures grouped by game.
- Keep cached dashboard, trophy, and capture data available for faster reloads.
- Save cached captures to the gallery and share them with other apps.

To load your data, BetterTrophies uses an NPSSO token from your PlayStation
account. The app focuses on trophies and captures rather than storefront or
purchase features.

BetterTrophies is not affiliated with or endorsed by Sony.
